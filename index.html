About Us

We are a group of students who formed a not-for-profit organization to teach your elementary schoolers currently on English as a Second Language. In our lessons, we include interactive lesson plans along with fun videos for your children to learn while also having lots of opportunities to socialize! On their learning journey, they will make friends, learn language arts, and more!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this 100% free?

    Yes, we are a non-profit organization, which means that our program is 100% free of charge.

  • What platform do you use for classes?

    We use Zoom to host class and Edmodo for homework.

  • Are you hiring volunteers to help?

    Yes, we are open to hiring students of all races from grades 7+ to help with our organization.

  • What do you teach?

    As of currently, we teach English for ages 4-6 and Elementary English for ages 7-10.

  • How many classes do you have?

    We currently have 4 classes, we are hoping to expand the number in the following month.

  • Do we need to buy any textbooks for students?

    No, all files are in Edmodo for students to print out.

  • How long are your classes?

    The Elementary English classes are 45 minutes. The younger class for ages 4-6 are 30 minutes.

  • Is there homework for students?

    Yes, every week there will be homework posted. In the following week, we will go over homework in class.

  • How often are your classes?

    We have all classes once per week. Different classes have different schedules.

  • Is there any weeks where there aren’t classes?

    The only week we won’t have class is the week of Christmas.

    Use the chat feature or send an email to us through the contact me option!
